About Us

Hi, I'm Fahmida, the founder of Modest Layers. My passion for creating inclusive, modest fashion stems from a love of empowering individuals to feel confident and stylish while staying true to their values! Modest Layers delivers stylish, versatile modest wear that blends full coverage with modern elegance. Our timeless, comfortable pieces are designed for those seeking inclusive and chic fashion options! We believe our mission is in celebrating individuality while offering styles that empower confidence. Our goal is to redefine modest fashion with timeless designs and uncompromising quality.

Meet Our Team

Our Story

The idea for Modest Layers was born out of a desire to create fashionable clothing that reflects values and individuality. We noticed a lack of options that balanced modesty with contemporary design, and decided to fill that gap with thoughtfully curated pieces for every occasion.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We design for all body types, ages, and cultural backgrounds.
  • Empowerment: Helping our customers feel confident in what they wear.
  • Sustainability: Committed to ethical fashion practices and quality materials.

What We Offer

At Modest Layers, we provide a wide selection of elegant abayas and hijabs designed to meet your fashion needs while ensuring comfort and versatility.
